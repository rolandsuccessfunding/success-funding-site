Frequently Asked Questions
  • How fast can I get funded?
    In many cases, within 1–5 business days for unsecured loans, and 7–21 for SBA or secured options.
  • Do I need perfect credit?
    No. We work with a range of credit profiles and match you with the best-fit product.
  • Do I need to provide collateral?
    Only for secured loan products like SBA or acquisition funding. We offer unsecured options as well.
  • Is there a minimum revenue requirement?
    Most working capital programs require at least $10,000/month in gross revenue.
